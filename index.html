AI Economic Identity

Native support for AI agents to hold assets, execute payments, and participate in economic activities autonomously without human intermediaries.

Micropayments

Enable granular, high-frequency transactions for API calls, computing resources, and algorithmic services with minimal overhead.

Gasless Transfers

EIP-2612 Permit and EIP-3009 support for meta-transactions, enabling authorization-based transfers without gas costs.
